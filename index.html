Our Methodology

A comprehensive six-phase approach that transforms abstract vision into concrete reality, ensuring every detail serves the human experience.

1

Discovery & Site Analysis

We begin by understanding your vision and analyzing the physical space to ensure a solid foundation.

  • Project brief discussion & detailed questionnaire
  • Comprehensive site measurements & survey
  • Sourcing requirements & feasibility check
2

Concept & Spatial Planning

We establish the functional layout and the aesthetic direction of the project through initial layouts and mood boards.

  • AutoCAD furniture layouts & appliance planning
  • Curated mood boards to define the design language
  • Reference imagery for all zones
  • Material charts to visualize the preliminary palette
3

3D Visualization

We bring the concept to life with photorealistic visuals, allowing you to see the space before execution begins.

  • First proposal 3D views for key areas
  • Collaborative discussion & changes implementation
  • Refinement of design elements
  • Final 3D walkthroughs & rendered views
4

Material & Fixtures Selection

A comprehensive curation phase where we finalize every texture, fixture, and loose furniture piece.

  • Surface finishes: Woodwork, flooring, ceiling & stone
  • Lighting & plumbing fixture specifications
  • Appliance selection (Built-ins, HVAC, AV)
  • Loose furniture & upholstery fabric curation
5

Technical Documentation & Handover

We translate the design into executable blueprints and finalized costs, readying the project for construction.

  • Comprehensive technical drawings (Electrical, Plumbing, Joinery)
  • Door, window, & wall elevation schedules
  • Detailed BOQ with material sizes & quantities
  • Contractor finalization & Design Handover
6

Construction & Execution

We oversee the physical transformation of the site, managing vendors and quality control to ensure the design intent is realized.

  • Site mobilization & project timeline management
  • Procurement coordination & vendor supervision
  • On-site execution (Civil, MEP, Carpentry & Finishes)
  • Furniture installation, art placement & styling
  • Final quality check (snag list) & site handover

Our Story

Svelte Studio was born from a belief that well-designed spaces have the power to quietly transform everyday life. Founded with a passion for thoughtful design and meticulous execution, the studio brings together creativity, functionality, and an unwavering attention to detail.

Founded by Preksha Jain, Svelte Studio reflects her deep appreciation for design that is both intuitive and intentional. With a background rooted in understanding space, structure, and human behavior, Preksha believes that great interiors go beyond trends—they respond to how people truly live. Her approach is hands-on and detail-driven, ensuring every project feels considered, personal, and enduring.

We approach every project as a collaboration—listening closely, understanding lifestyles, and translating individual stories into spaces that feel timeless yet lived-in. From concept to completion, our process is seamless and transparent, supported by trusted vendors and craftsmen.

At Svelte Studio, design is never just about aesthetics. It’s about creating homes that evolve with you, reflect who you are, and support the way you live—beautifully, effortlessly, and with intention.
